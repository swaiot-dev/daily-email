Welcome to [Daily Email]!

[Daily Email] is an innovative tool designed to help users manage their Gmail inbox efficiently, offering features such as email categorization, and optimized search.

Key Features

  • Email Categorization: Automatically organize emails into folders based on keywords or sender information.
  • Quick Email Search: Find the right email with advanced search capabilities.
  • Email Notification: Automatic notification of new emails.

Permission Details

To enable these features, we require your authorization to access specific Gmail data:

  • Read Emails (read-only): For email categorization.

We strictly adhere to the principle of minimal permissions and only access the data required for service delivery.

Data Privacy and Security
